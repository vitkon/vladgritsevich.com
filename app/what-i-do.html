What I Do

All furniture deserves a second life. My goal is to not meet your expectations but to exceed them. I have a 20 year experience in design and upholstery, both domestic and commercial work as well as vast knowledge of materials, fabrics and tools.

No job is too odd and no piece of furniture is too old. I believe in quality not quantity so your piece will get special treatment and attention it deserves.

Currently living and expanding my client base in Northern New Jersey. What I do is a one-man job. I don’t rush to complete the project yet I’m efficient and will almost always provide you with a fair dead-line date. Pricing is discussed based on the complexity of the work, budget, materials and other factors.
